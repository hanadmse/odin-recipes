Pad Thai Recipe

Description

Enjoy Vegetarian Pad Thai, a vibrant stir-fry of rice noodles with vegetables and tofu, coated in a tangy tamarind sauce, topped with crushed peanuts and lime.

Ingredients

  • Rice noodles
  • Tofu, cubed/li>
  • Egg (optional)
  • Carrot, julienned
  • Bell pepper, sliced
  • Bean sprouts
  • Green onions, chopped
  • Garlic, minced
  • Peanuts, crushed

Recipe Steps

  1. Soak rice noodles in warm water until soft.
  2. Fry tofu in oil until golden, set aside.
  3. In the same pan, add garlic and vegetables, stir-fry until just tender.
  4. Add noodles, tamarind paste, soy sauce, and brown sugar; toss well.
  5. Mix in tofu and bean sprouts.
  6. Serve topped with peanuts, green onions, and a lime wedge.
