Chicken Tikka Masala

Description

Indulge in Chicken Tikka Masala, a creamy, spiced Indian dish with yogurt-marinated chicken simmered in a tomato-based sauce, perfect alongside basmati rice or naan.

Ingredients

  • Chicken breasts, cubed
  • Yogurt
  • Garlic, minced
  • Ginger, minced
  • Tomato puree
  • Cream
  • Onion, diced
  • Garam masala
  • Cumin
  • Coriander
  • Chili powder
  • Salt
  • Oil
  • Cilantro, for garnish

Recipe Steps

  1. Marinate chicken in yogurt, garlic, ginger, and spices.
  2. Saut√© onions in oil until translucent.
  3. Add marinated chicken and cook until browned.
  4. Pour in tomato puree and simmer.
  5. Stir in cream, adjust seasonings, and simmer until thickened.
  6. Garnish with cilantro and serve.
