Lasagna Recipe

Description

Discover our classic Lasagna recipe with a step-by-step guide and expert tips.

Perfect for beginners and seasoned chefs alike, this webpage offers everything you need to create a delicious, comforting meal for any occasion

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 24 ounces ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tablespoons parsley, chopped
  • 3 cups mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • 2 cups Parmesan cheese, grated
  • 28 ounces tomato sauce
  • 12 lasagna noodles, cooked
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Olive oil

Recipe Steps

  1. Preheat Oven: Set to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Brown Meat: In a skillet, sauté onion and garlic, add ground beef, cook until browned, then drain.
  3. Simmer Sauce: Add tomato sauce to the meat, season, and simmer for 15 minutes.
  4. Mix Cheese: Combine ricotta, egg, parsley, 1 cup mozzarella, and 1 cup Parmesan.
  5. Assemble Lasagna: Layer in a 9x13 inch dish: sauce, noodles, ricotta mix, mozzarella, and Parmesan; repeat.
  6. Bake: Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove foil, bake another 25 minutes until golden.
  7. Rest and Serve: Let cool for 10 minutes before serving.
